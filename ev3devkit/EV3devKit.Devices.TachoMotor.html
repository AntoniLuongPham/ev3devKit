TachoMotor

Object Hierarchy:

Description:

public class TachoMotor : Device

Represents a motor with tachometer feedback.

The EV3 Large and Medium and NXT motors are this type of motor.


Namespace: EV3devKit.Devices
Package: ev3devkit
