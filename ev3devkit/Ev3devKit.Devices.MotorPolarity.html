MotorPolarity

Description:

public enum MotorPolarity

The polarity of a motor. In other words, which direction of rotation is positive.

The direction of rotation (clockwise or counter-clockwise) is determined by viewing the motor with the shaft end facing you.


Namespace: Ev3devKit.Devices
Package: ev3devkit

Content:

Enum values:

  • NORMAL - Clockwise is considered the positive direction.
  • INVERTED - Counter- clockwise is considered the positive direction.
