WidgetAlign

Description:

public enum WidgetAlign

Specifies how a Widget should be laid out in a Container.


Namespace: EV3devKit.UI
Package: ev3devKit

Content:

Enum values:

  • FILL - The widget should fill the entire container.
  • START - The widget should be aligned to the start (top or left) of the container.
  • CENTER - The widget should be aligned to the center (middle) of the container.
  • END - The widget should be aligned to the end (bottom or right) of the container.
